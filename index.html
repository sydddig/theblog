Hello and welcome to my F1 blog ! My name is Sydni and I am a 2nd year design student studying at USFCA. This blog is here to help newer fans learn a little bit more about F1, and for older fans, just a fun little blog to read if you love the sport. As a queer woc fan, I know how unsafe and uninviting the fandom can be at times, if not all the time. I want to offer a safe space for people like me, or not even like me, but just want to get away from all the drama that is, unfortunately, the F1 fandom. It's here for us to chill and relax. People take F1 so seriously, and while I do, it's just a hobby at the end of the day, and people should be able to enjoy it without feeling like they need to know EVERY little detail, like not everyone needs to know the exact hospital Fernando Alonso was born at and what doctor delivered him. I'm just going to go over a little bit about what F1 is, my favorite drivers, some drama perhaps, and honestly, whatever else I feel like talking about. I hope whoever ends up reading this enjoys this blog as much as I enjoyed making it !
